# PiggyWise (หมูทอง)

**AI-Powered Portfolio Management System for Thai Investors**

PiggyWise sells Google Sheets templates that act as a personal finance coach: automated monthly rebalancing (DCA), psychological guardrails, and an AI Prompt Library that turns any AI (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Copilot) into the customer's personal financial advisor. All data stays in the customer's own Google Drive — PiggyWise has no backend.

Full business blueprint: [`docs/PiggyWise_master_blueprint.md`](docs/PiggyWise_master_blueprint.md) (Thai)

---

## Products

Three Google Sheets templates, one per target audience:

| Package | Strategy | Allocation | Price (Standard / Premium) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Retire Smooth** (เกษียณสบาย) | Conservative, 20-yr compounding | VOO 60% / BND 30% / Gold 10% | ฿390 / ฿790 |
| **Hybrid Wealth** (สมดุล) | Growth + dividends, two-phase | VOO 45% / QQQM 40% / SCHD 15% | ฿490 / ฿890 |
| **Aggressive Go** (สายซิ่ง) | High-risk with crypto guardrails | VOO/QQQM 70% / BTC 20% / Alt 10% | ฿590 / ฿990 |

**Standard** = customer self-setup via Force Copy link + PDF guide (0 creator hours).  
**Premium** = creator inputs customer data into master sheet (5 min) + 30-min onboarding call + 30-day email support.

---

## Delivery

- **Standard:** Payment → auto-send PDF → customer clicks `/copy` link → Google Sheet cloned into their Drive.
- **Premium:** Customer fills Requirements Form → creator populates `DCA_Plan` + `README` tabs → shares `/copy` link → call + 30-day support.
