# research

Research topics, themes, and market data using web search.

## Input
- Theme name (e.g., "1970s") or research question

## Output
- Structured list of findings relevant to the goal
- Sources noted where important

## Usage
Use the WebSearch tool to find information. Summarize findings clearly and concisely.

## For Theme Research (brain-health use case)
1. Search for the most iconic/memorable aspects of the era
2. Identify 10 diverse topics that cover: music, TV, movies, events, fashion, food, sports, technology, culture, language
3. Verify topics are well-known enough for seniors who lived through the era
4. Avoid obscure or controversial references
5. Output: ranked list of 10 topics with brief description of each
